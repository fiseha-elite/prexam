The Drafting Architecture

Use the "PEEL" method for every paragraph to ensure maximum clarity and impact:

  • P: Point (The main idea)
  • E: Evidence (Facts/Quotes)
  • E: Explanation (Why it matters)
  • L: Link (Transition to next point)
