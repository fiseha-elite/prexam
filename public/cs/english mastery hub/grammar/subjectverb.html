Subject-Verb Agreement

A simple concept that gets complicated in academic writing. Let's master it.

Rule: The Distraction Trap

Don't let words that come between the subject and the verb confuse you. Mentally cross out the middle stuff.

"The set of technical books is on the table."

The subject is set (singular), not books. Therefore, the verb must be is.

Collective Nouns

Words like Team, Faculty, or Committee are usually singular in academic English.

Compound Subjects

Subjects joined by "and" usually take a plural verb.

Elite Instinct Test
"The list of participants _______ still growing."
