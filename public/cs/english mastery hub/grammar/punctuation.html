The Semicolon Masterclass

It’s not just a comma with a hat. It’s a tool for connecting powerful ideas.

Rule #1: Connecting Independent Clauses

Use a semicolon to join two complete sentences that are closely related in thought. Never use it if one side isn't a full sentence.

❌ The Amateur I love studying English, it is my favorite subject. (This is a Comma Splice)
✅ The Elite I love studying English; it is my favorite subject.

Rule #2: The Complex List

When list items already contain commas, use semicolons to separate the groups so the reader doesn't get lost.

✅ Pro Example I have lived in Paris, France; Tokyo, Japan; and London, England.

Quick Check

Fix this sentence: "The sun was setting, the air grew cold."
